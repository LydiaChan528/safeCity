ABOUT



Our motto is "Creating a safer city, one step at a time". We believe that safe walks and routes home should be available to all. Safe City's GPS allows users to navigate around high crime density areas and indicates the level of safety the calculated route has. An additional feature is filtering by gender; by using this filter, users are able to get more specific and pertinent results.
